About Us

Welcome to our online puzzle store! We are a company that was founded by a puzzle enthusiast who loves nothing more than creating puzzles as a way to unwind and decompress from the stress of daily life.
Our founder, Damian V, has been passionate about puzzles for as long as they can remember. From jigsaw puzzles to crosswords and Sudoku, they have always found joy in solving puzzles and have been fascinated by the process of creating them.

Over time, Damian V discovered that creating puzzles was not only a fun hobby but also an effective way to reduce stress and improve their overall well-being. As a result, they decided to turn their passion into a business, with the goal of sharing their love of puzzles with others and helping them experience the same benefits.

About company

At our online puzzle store, you will find a wide variety of puzzles to choose from, ranging from traditional jigsaw puzzles to brain teasers and logic games.

Our puzzles are designed to challenge and engage your mind, helping you improve your problem-solving skills, memory, and overall cognitive function.
